A day with all 20 active

  1. 7:00a — Digest #20 lands in menu-bar: 2 need you, 1 overdue, starter question.
  2. Open app — Command Bar #1 (⌘K) is the entry; or Home.
  3. Morning ritual #9 — guided: Health #2 → Needs you #10 → pick top 3 → sweep Capture.
  4. Do now from Attention Graph; click → Focus with item; split view #5 shows the note beside it.
  5. Hit a blocker → companion #4 drafts an update; related rail #14 shows the auth note.
  6. Thought hits → hotkey #8 (or ⌘K) → Dump; smart link #7 suggests VAS.
  7. Meeting ends → paste → Meeting pipeline; bulk-create tasks; drag #12 one onto a project.
  8. After 2 days away → Activity #3 “since Tuesday” resume; digest #20 primed you.
  9. Weekly → ritual #9; energy #17 says protect 9–11a; calendar #16 blocks it.
  10. Switch to personal life → spaces #19; ADHD-calm #18 for evenings.

Experience principles the 20 enforce

  • One door per job — Command Bar drives; hotkey captures; Needs-you aggregates.
  • The app notices drift — Health, Activity, Digest, Rituals.
  • Context follows you — Companion, Related rail, Split view.
  • Friction dies — Smart links, Drag, Bulk, Templates.
  • Structure is optional until useful — Capture dump, safe promote.
  • Local first, cloud labeled — LM Studio → Gemini.
